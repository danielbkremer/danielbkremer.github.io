Daniel Kremer

Daniel Kremer is the director of the critically acclaimed epic thriller Overwhelm the Sky (2019), and the author of Sidney J. Furie: Life and Films (2015) and Adventures in Auteurism (2025). He is also a prolific contributor of audio commentaries and video essays, which have appeared on 200+ physical media releases for The Criterion Collection, Kino Lorber, Imprint Films, Severin, Arrow, and many other labels. His full list of credits is available on IMDb.

Daniel's work sits at the intersection of hands-on feature-film directing and film history, with a special focus on overlooked and unjustly neglected directors and films.

His forthcoming book, My Lunches With Henry Jaglom: A Biographical Dialogue, is due out in March 2026.
